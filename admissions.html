  • Annual cultural exchange residencies and pen-pal programmes.

Career & Scholarship Navigation

Advisors co-create actionable roadmaps, aligning strengths with university and scholarship pathways worldwide.

STEM & Robotics Studio

Future-ready labs that integrate coding, engineering, and creative problem solving.
